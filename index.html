Why Thermal Label Studio?

⚡

One-Click Tab Capture

Extract and format shipping labels instantly from Pirate Ship, Shopify, eBay, and UPS. Click "Capture Tab" to grab, align, and prepare pages for printing without downloading.

📶

Bluetooth & Serial Engines

Cut the cords. Supports standard Web Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) and high-speed Bluetooth Classic / USB Serial configurations for zero-latency printing.

🤖

Auto-Protocol Handshake

No complex UUID code configuration or manual setup required. The print engine automatically negotiates and detects printer specifications (FEE7 / WeChat vs ISSC / 4953 protocols).

⚙️

Real-Time Canvas Tuning

Preview and tune labels before printing. Adjust monochrome density thresholds, invert print colors, or flip print direction 180° for upside-down layouts.

📂

Automatic Interceptor

Detects and captures thermal label PDF downloads automatically, opening them directly in your side panel for immediate dispatch.

🖱️

Context Menu Printing

Right-click any PDF label link or invoice image on any webpage to send it directly to the thermal print queue.

First-Time Setup Guide

1

Install the Extension

Add the extension from the Chrome Web Store, then click the puzzle piece (🧩) in your toolbar and Pin Thermal Label Studio for quick access.

2

Connect your Printer

Turn on your printer and pair it in your operating system's Bluetooth settings (or attach the USB cable). Click Select / Change Printer under section 3 inside the Side Panel to grant Chrome permission.

3

Extract & Print

Drag and drop a 4x6 label PDF or click Capture Tab to fetch one. Hit **Connect & Print** to print wireslessly!

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

At Thermal Label Studio, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data within the extension context.

1. Data Collection & Privacy

We do not collect, store, or transmit your private data, documents, or shipping labels.

  • Local Rendering: All PDF parsing, image thresholding, rotation, and TSPL bitmap compilation are performed 100% locally inside your browser's extension sandbox environment. No document data is sent to external servers.
  • Printer Data: Printer Bluetooth device names and Serial identifiers are stored locally on your device via Chrome Local Storage only to maintain your session. We do not track print content or device specifics.

2. Permissions Justification

The extension requests the following browser permissions solely to enable print workflows:

  • downloads: Used to intercept label PDF file downloads from carrier sites so they are opened directly in your printing side panel.
  • sidePanel: Renders a sidebar utility so you can format and dispatch prints alongside your shipping portal page.
  • contextMenus: Enables right-click shortcuts to route links or images directly to the print preview.
  • Host Permissions (e.g. <all_urls>): Required to fetch PDF raw files from carrier domains (UPS, FedEx, DHL, Shopify) to bypass browser CORS (Cross-Origin Resource Sharing) restrictions.

3. Payment & Licensing Data

We use ExtensionPay (integrated with Stripe) to handle product licensing and payments. When you purchase a premium license:

  • ExtensionPay checks and validates your license status using secure tokens mapped to your logged-in Google Chrome profile.
  • Credit card details, emails, and transaction operations are securely processed and hosted by Stripe. We do not view, collect, or store your credit card or financial data.

4. Support & Contact

If you have any questions, encounter issues, or require assistance, please contact support via the GitHub repository issues page or at support@extensionpay.com.
